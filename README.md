# GoogleMeetBackgroundDesign

## How to add these backgrounds to Google Meet

### Before joining a meeting
1. Open [Google Meet](https://meet.google.com).
2. On the home screen, click your camera preview.
3. Click **Apply visual effects** (the sparkle/star icon).
4. Under **Backgrounds**, click the **+ Add** button.
5. Select a PNG file from the `images/` folder in this repo.
6. Click the uploaded thumbnail to apply it — you'll see the effect in your camera preview.
7. Join the meeting as usual.

### During an active meeting
1. Click the **three-dot menu (More options)** in the meeting controls.
2. Select **Apply visual effects**.
3. Under **Backgrounds**, click **+ Add**.
4. Upload a PNG from `images/` and click it to apply.

### Notes
- Backgrounds work best with good, even lighting on your face.
- Google Meet requires a browser (Chrome, Edge, or the Meet desktop/mobile app) that supports virtual backgrounds — very old browsers or low-powered devices may not show the option.
- Custom backgrounds are stored per Google account, not per device — once uploaded, they'll be available next time you sign in from anywhere.
- To remove a background, go back to **Apply visual effects → Backgrounds** and select **None**.
